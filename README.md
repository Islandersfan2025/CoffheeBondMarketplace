# CoffheeBondMarketplace
Demo of Coffhee Finance's bond trading platform.

1. The Confidential Hub: Arbitrum SepoliaThe "Hub" acts as the central vault and the primary source of truth for all bond issuances. It is where the ERC-3475 "Golden Copy" of each bond resides.Fhenix CoFHE (Coprocessor): The core engine that enables "Confidentiality-in-Use." Unlike standard blockchains where every transaction is public, CoFHE allows the platform to calculate interest, verify collateral, and manage redemptions on encrypted data.Encrypted State Storage: Sensitive variables—such as an institution's total debt exposure or a specific holder's principal amount—are stored as encrypted euint types.The Frappucino Vault: A specialized smart contract that handles the "wrapping" of complex ERC-3475 bonds into simpler ERC-1155 receipts for cross-chain movement.2. The Liquid Spokes: Cross-Chain LiquidityTo maximize reach, Coffhee does not force users to trade on the high-security Hub. Instead, it "provisions" liquidity to "Spoke" chains where retail users and DeFi protocols already live.ERC-1155 Receipts: These are "liquid shadows" of the underlying bond. They are standard, non-encrypted tokens that represent ownership of a specific bond tranche.Supported Spokes (2026):Base & Robinhood Chain: Targeted at retail investors for easy access and low fees.Arc (Circle): Utilized for institutional-grade settlement using native USDC.Chainlink CCIP Integration: The "Highway" that connects the Hub to the Spokes. CCIP handles the secure burning of a receipt on one chain and its minting on another, ensuring that no two chains can claim the same bond simultaneously.3. Automation & Oracle LayerThe platform eliminates the need for manual "back-office" administrative work through decentralized automation.Chainlink CRE (Coprocessor Runtime Environment): Acts as a decentralized scheduler. It monitors bond maturity dates and automatically triggers the executePayout() logic on the Hub the moment a bond expires.Real-Time Data Feeds: For variable-rate or inflation-linked bonds, the infrastructure pulls live CPI or interest rate data to adjust bond coupons homomorphically (inside the encrypted environment).4. The Terminal Interface (User Layer)The user interaction layer is designed to look and feel like a professional Linux Terminal, signaling a shift from "DeFi toys" to "Institutional Tools."Permissioned Decryption (sealoutput): When a holder wants to view their private balance, the Terminal UI requests a "Sealed Output" from the FHE coprocessor. This data is re-encrypted for the user's specific public key, ensuring that only their screen can decrypt and display the private financial data.Unified Command Line: A single interface where users can list, buy, wrap, and bridge bonds across all supported chains without ever leaving the terminal environment.Infrastructure SummaryLayerComponentFunctionExecutionFhenix CoFHEEncrypted math and privacy-preserving logic.Asset StandardERC-3475Multi-class bond management in a single contract.InteroperabilityChainlink CCIPMoving bond receipts between Arbitrum and Spoke chains.SettlementT+0 Atomic SwapsInstant exchange of bond receipts for stablecoins.
